    Jayawant Shikshan Prasarak Mandal is a charitable educational trust, imparting quality professional education in the field of Engineering, Pharmacy, Management, Computer Application etc. JSPM has established four campuses in Pune at Tathawade, Hadapsar, Wagholi and Bavdhan. Each campus consists of institutes of global competitiveness. All Institutes/Colleges of JSPM are approved by AICTE, recognized by Govt. of Maharashtra and are affiliated to University of Pune.

Mission Statement
"To provide, nurture and maintain an environment of high academic excellence, research and entrepreneurship for all aspiring students, which will prepare them to face global challenges maintaining high ethical and moral standards."

Vision Statement
"To satisfy the aspirations of youth force, who want to lead our nation towards prosperity through technoeconomic development."

Dr. M.G. Jadhav
Principal Jscoe, Hadapsar Pune
Prof. (Dr.) M.G.Jadhav graduated in Mechanical Engineering(Distinction) in 1979. He completed his M.E. by Research from Shivaji University in Mechanical Engineering in 1992 and Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Shivaji University in 1997.He had worked in Industry for a period of 5 years. He then opted educational field and is serving this noble profession for the last 26 years.

During his academic tenure,he had received Y.P. Powar Prize from Shivaji University in 1977 and Wanson Thermax Prize in 1978. Dr. M.G. Jadhav had been in Technical Teaching field since 1984. He worked as Professor and HOD of Mechanical Engineering at W. I. T. Solapur. There he undertook Energy Park Project under MNRE Govt of India,New Delhi.He was the founder of M. E. (Mech.- Design Engg.) course.His dedication and devotion became fruitful when Mechanical Engineering Dept at W.I.T. got N.B.A. accreditation. He organised URJA 2K7 paper presentation competition at national level. Dr.M.G.Jadhav worked as Principal of P.G.M.C.O.E. Pune. There he was the Organizing Chairman of National Conference sponsored by University of Pune.He also organized Technomeet in 2008 and 2009.

He was Chairman B.O.S Mechanical Engineering, Member of Faculty of Engineeirng, Member R. R. Committee at Shivaji University Kolhapur, and Solapur University, Solapur. He is officiating as Examiner & Paper setter at UG/PG of C.O.E.P., Shivaji University & Solapur University.He works as Resource Person for S.T.T.P., the member of L.I.C. & Staff Selection Committee at various colleges.

Dr. M.G. Jadhav has published 10 research papers in National/International refreed journals and presented 9 papers in National/International Conferences. He is recognised guide for Ph.D. (Mechanical Engineering). He has guided M.E. dessertations and students are undergoing Ph.D. reserch. His contribution in this sphere of education field doesn't halt here.He is always in pursuit of new trends for inculcating new values and added technical learning for the students.
